Who We Are

mediAccess is a health-tech initiative designed to give individuals complete control over their medical data. We are committed to a future where health information is accessible, secure, and shared only with consent — across hospitals, insurers, and public health systems.

For Patients

Patients can access and manage their health records, link their ABHA ID, add dependents, and share reports securely with doctors, hospitals, and insurers. Your data is protected, encrypted, and fully consent-driven.
